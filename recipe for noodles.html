RECIPE FOR MAKING NOODLES

  • First Step

    • Take 2 cups of water in sauce and boil it in medium high flame.

  • Second Step

    • Take a packet of noodle and put it in the sauce pan and add the seasoning and wait for it to cook.

  • Third Step

    • chop some carrots, chillies and capsicum and put in the sauce pan.

  • Fourth Step

    • Also add some peas, some chilli sauce and vineger in the noodles and just wait for the peas to cook.

  • Last Step

    • Now just pour the noodles in the plate and enjoy your delicious noodles.
